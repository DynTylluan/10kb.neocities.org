Hannah Höch

One of the things I dislike about featuring real–world artists is how I never get a sense of who the artist was underneath whatever works they were fortunate enough to get published. There’s no DeviantART or other blog for me to judge all of their works on, so I have to drag my ass to some obscure, bootleg, poorly–written and boring–as–all–get–out online art gallery and then drag an opinion out of my throat like a ferret trying to escape the pants they were trapped in. So here’s Hannah Höch, another artist who joins the weird “o” club after Martinus Rørbye, and a creator of some bloody disturbing stuff. Also, she was bisexual. Brilliant!
